Mission Statement

Our mission is the revelation of God's anointing within His people's past, present and future.Though we are sinners, God has granted us His grace, and thus we are reconciled with Him and with each other.

Blessings

While honoring God's blessings given to those before us, we receive new blessings today.

Gospel

We are vessels through which His Gospel preaches, heals and continually defends against the temptation of our sinful nature.

Ministry

We plant the seeds to continuously support and build the ministry's tomorrow with each new generation.

Leadership

Meet our leaders!

Lightning

Visionary Board Committee

The purpose of Lightning is to oversee the ministry’s overall mission in the current and future state, and to ensure that certain tasks aligned to the mission statement are appropriately identified, measured, monitored and controlled within the governance process managed by Lightning. Lightning members are nominated and approved by the Pastor.

Thunder

Administrative Board Committee

The purpose of Thunder is to manage the tasks and activities aligned to the ministry's mission statement. Such tasks and activities are designed for the benefit of the congregation by promoting fellowship and awareness. New Thunder members are nominated by Lightning, recommended to existing Thunder members, and appointed by the congregation by election, which is held annually on the first Sunday of June, or as needed.
